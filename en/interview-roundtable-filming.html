Available formats

For every need,
the right setup

01
Face-camera interview
1 or 2 speakers. Tight framing, 3-point lighting, neutral or branded background. Ideal format for client testimonials, executive portraits, HR communications.
02
2-person debate
2 speakers face to face. 3 axes: each face + wide shot. Crossed wireless mics. Perfect for live Q&A formats on LinkedIn.
03
Roundtable
4 to 8 participants around a table. Wide angle + motorised cameras for tight shots. Ideal for industry events, expert panels, congresses.
04
TV set
Set design, animated graphics, teleprompter, remote speakers as tiles. See the corporate live show page.
05
Mobile interview
Light kit (1-2 cameras, lapel mic, portable LED). 30-minute setup. For quick interviews or event reporting.
06
Social Media Live
Live broadcast on your professional platforms (YouTube, LinkedIn, X, TikTok, Instagram). Format optimised for B2B organic engagement.

The process

From booking to file delivery

01
Brief & recce
Discussion on speakers, desired format (interview, debate, roundtable), brand identity. Technical recce when possible.
02
Setup in 2h
Set operational before the first attendees: dedicated cameras, HF mics, 3-point lighting per speaker, neutral or branded background.
03
Multi-angle capture
Camera switching, real-time graphics (lower-thirds, captions), sound mixing. Live TV-style direction from the control room.
04
File delivered the same day
Full HD or 4K on request. Secure link available as soon as the event ends, ready to archive, edit or rebroadcast.
