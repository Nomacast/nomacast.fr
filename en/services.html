Frequently asked

The 3 questions I get the most

Which setup should I choose for my event?

If you have a classic event (conference, seminar, AGM) on a single site, go with Conference & seminar. For events with high brand stakes (product launch, premium hybrid AGM), look at Corporate live show. For multiple simultaneous sites, it's Multi-site live streaming. For interview/roundtable, Interview & roundtable. If you wish to broadcast on multiple platforms in parallel, add Multi-platform streaming as an option. I can also guide you on request.

Are prices fixed or variable?

The entry price for Conference & seminar in Paris is €1,500 HT. For other formats (live show, multiplex, etc.), the quote is custom-tailored based on complexity, number of sites and duration. You can estimate in 30 seconds or request a custom quote within 24h.

How far in advance should I contact you?

Ideally 2 to 4 weeks ahead for a standard setup (Conference, Interview). For a live show or multiplex, plan 3 to 6 weeks since preparation is more demanding. Same-day under 24h is possible if the date is available.
